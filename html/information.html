Cinnamon House

"Cinnamon House" is a cozy cabin located in the beautiful region of Mindo, Ecuador. This countryside retreat has all the modern amenities you need, such as a fully equipped kitchen, private bathroom, and Wi-Fi, but it also maintains the rustic and natural charm of its surroundings with wooden details and warm decoration. In addition, its location allows you to enjoy the wonders of the surrounding nature, with hiking trails, rivers, and nearby waterfalls, as well as a great variety of native bird and animal species. The experience at "Cinnamon House" is ideal for those looking to disconnect from the urban hustle and connect with the tranquility and natural beauty of Ecuador.

Address: Colibrie Street, Mindo, Ecuador, 170167.

The Temple I

El The Temple I, Mindo in Mindo ofrece alojamiento de 3 estrellas con centro de fitness, jardín y salón compartido. Este hotel de 3 estrellas cuenta con cocina compartida y mostrador de información turística. Hay aparcamiento privado gratuito y servicio de enlace con el aeropuerto por un suplemento. Las habitaciones tienen patio con vistas a la montaña. Las habitaciones del The Temple I, Mindo disponen de baño privado con ducha y WiFi gratuita. Algunas también cuentan con balcón. Todas las habitaciones disponen de armario y cafetera. Todas las mañanas se sirve un desayuno buffet, continental y vegano.
